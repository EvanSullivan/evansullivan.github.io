Evan Sullivan

Interface Designer

I define the success of a product as its ability to meet real human needs in an effortless and focused manner. By marrying the principles of both graphic and human-centered design, I create products that are useful, accessible, and desirable.

My work helps people prevent skin cancer, get tech help, and enjoy absurd fun. Learn more about selected projects below.
